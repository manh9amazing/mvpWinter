Who are we?

Proud to be students of class CI102-3 of Drexel University, we are passionate young men from all over the world, who want to contribute our ability to create a great project for everyone. The members are Andy, Mike, and double Dat (Dat D. and Dat Ng.)

What is our project?

Nowadays, everyone, especially young people have troubles managing their money, leading to potential obnoxious consequences sooner or later. We are fascinated by the issue and feel the responsibility to mitigate the problem. And through days of thinking, we think of this website as our final solution. We genuinely hope, with our helps, anyone using our website can have a happy life. With their happy wallet.

We want to hear from you!
